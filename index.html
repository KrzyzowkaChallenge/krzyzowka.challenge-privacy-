1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Krzyżówka Challenge mobile application ("Application") developed by Paweł Grzejszczak ("Service Provider", "we", "us"), residing in Poland. The Application is offered as an ad-supported free service.

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / RODO) and applicable Polish law.

Key facts at a glance: Your gameplay progress, settings, coins, and avatar choices are stored only on your device. We do not operate our own servers. The only data leaving your device is collected automatically by third-party SDKs (Firebase, AdMob) described below.

2. What data is collected

Data stored locally on your device only (not transmitted to us or any server):

  • Game progress, level history, coin balance
  • Avatar and customisation choices
  • Application settings (sound, preferences)

Data collected automatically by third-party SDKs on our behalf:

Category Examples Collected by Purpose
Device identifiers Device ID, Advertising ID Firebase, AdMob Analytics, advertising
App interactions Screens visited, button taps, session duration Firebase Analytics App improvement
Diagnostics & crash logs Crash reports, error logs, device OS version Firebase Crashlytics Bug fixing
Advertising data Ad interactions, ad impressions AdMob Displaying ads

The Application does not collect: precise location data, contacts, photos, microphone or camera input, financial information, or health data.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal data.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6)

We rely on the following legal bases to process personal data:

  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — processing by Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix technical errors, which is necessary for the stable operation of the Application.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — processing by Firebase Analytics and AdMob for analytics and personalised advertising. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Your rights).

For users under the age of 16, consent must be given or authorised by a parent or legal guardian (see Minors section).

4. Third-party services

The Application integrates the following third-party SDKs, each governed by its own Privacy Policy:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Data processed by these services is transferred to and stored on Google's servers, which may be located outside the European Economic Area. Google LLC participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and applies standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

We may also disclose data when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

5. Minors & parental consent

The Application is designed for users aged 13 and above. However, under GDPR Article 8 and Polish law, users under the age of 16 require the consent of a parent or legal guardian for any processing of personal data based on consent (e.g. analytics, personalised ads).

For parents and guardians: If your child is under 16 and uses this Application, please review this Privacy Policy together. If you believe your child has provided personal data without your authorisation, contact us at krzyzowka.challenge@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

6. Your rights under GDPR (Art. 15–22)

As a data subject in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of access (Art. 15) Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Right to rectification (Art. 16) Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Right to erasure (Art. 17) Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
Right to restriction (Art. 18) Request that we limit how we use your data.
Right to data portability (Art. 20) Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Right to object (Art. 21) Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
Right to withdraw consent Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
Right to lodge a complaint File a complaint with the Polish supervisory authority (UODO): uodo.gov.pl.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at krzyzowka.challenge@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Opt-out of data collection: You can disable personalised advertising in your device settings (Google Ads settings / Limit Ad Tracking). You can stop all SDK data collection by uninstalling the Application.

7. Data retention

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you uninstall the Application. We have no access to this data.

Data collected by Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics is retained for up to 24 months, after which it may be stored in anonymised aggregate form. AdMob data retention is governed by Google's own policies.

To request deletion of any data associated with your use of the Application, please contact us at krzyzowka.challenge@gmail.com.

8. Security

All data transmitted by third-party SDKs is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Local data on your device is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms. We strongly advise against jailbreaking or rooting your device, as this may compromise security and affect Application functionality.

While we and our SDK providers take reasonable measures to protect data, no system is entirely immune to security incidents.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Application after changes are posted constitutes acknowledgment of the updated policy.
